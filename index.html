Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? Here are the answers to the most common ones

Do you bring your own cleaning equipment?
No hoover, but yes – I bring my own basic sprays, cloths and supplies. I'm also very happy to use any cleaning products or equipment that you already have at home if you prefer. Just let me know!
How do I book?

There are three easy ways to book:

  • WhatsApp – message me with your preferred date and time and I'll reply quickly to confirm availability and get you booked in.
  • Online calendar – pick a slot that suits you directly at cal.com/hey-tricleans.
  • Call me – prefer to chat first? Give me a ring on +44 7786 817644.
How do I check your availability?

The quickest way is to check the calendar directly at cal.com/hey-tricleans — you'll be able to see open slots in real time.

If you have any questions about availability or need a slot that isn't showing, just WhatsApp me and I'll do my best to help you get booked in.

What areas do you cover?
I serve Peckham, Dulwich, Camberwell and all surrounding postcodes in South East London (SE15, SE21, SE5 and nearby areas).
What's your cancellation policy?
You can cancel or reschedule for free up to 24 hours before your clean. For shorter notice, just message me and I'll do my best to help.
